Our Mission

A University Built for Nigeria's Future

Located in the heart of Sango Ota, Ogun State, OCROWN University was established with a clear vision: to provide quality, accessible, and transformative higher education to Nigerian students and beyond.

We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and producing graduates who are ready to contribute meaningfully to society and the Nigerian economy.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world — and OCROWN is that weapon for every Nigerian student."
— Vice Chancellor, OCROWN University

Undergraduate Admissions

Your Journey Starts Here

OCROWN University welcomes dedicated students through the JAMB UTME process. Purchase your application form and begin your journey today.

Entry Requirements

✓JAMB UTME Score (minimum 180)
✓5 O'Level Credits incl. English & Maths
✓Post-UTME Screening
✓Direct Entry via A'Levels or ND

Application Form Fee

₦10,000
One-time, non-refundable fee
How to Apply
01
Register on JAMB Portal
Create a JAMB profile and choose OCROWN University as first or second choice.
02
Purchase Application Form
Pay ₦10,000 via bank transfer or card and receive your form.
03
Complete Your Application
Fill in your details, upload documents and submit your form.
04
Post-UTME Screening
Sit OCROWN's Post-UTME screening exercise on campus or online.
05
Admission & Registration
Accept your offer on JAMB CAPS, pay fees and complete registration.
